Hello friends!

Lately I've been learning about computer graphics. In particular, I've been working with pixel shaders; programs that run on the graphics card and rapidly calculate colors for each pixel on the screen. They are useful in 2D and 3D video games and simulations, or simply to generate intricate artwork.

Since the holidays are upon us, I put together a gallery of interactive pixel shader art to share with my friends! I hope you enjoy!

For a best experience, please use a desktop web browser. While most mobile devices should be capable of displaying the artwork, the interactivity works best with a mouse.

Please let me know if you have any questions or comments!
